Our Mission

Making cooking easier, one projection at a time

Why We Built Projecto Chef

Our mission is to make cooking easier and more accessible for everyone. We believe that traditional cookbooks and messy tablets shouldn't hold you back from creating amazing meals in your kitchen.

Our Story

We're a group of juniors in high school who love cooking but were constantly frustrated with the challenges of following recipes in the kitchen. Physical cookbooks get dirty and take up counter space, tablets are impossible to navigate with messy hands, and phone screens are just too small to read comfortably while cooking.

One day, while struggling to follow a recipe covered in flour, we had a lightbulb moment: what if we could project recipes directly onto the kitchen counter? No more switching between your hands and your device. No more squinting at tiny text. Just clear, hands-free recipe instructions exactly where you need them.

After months of brainstorming, prototyping, and testing in our own kitchens, Projecto Chef was born. We're incredibly proud to bring this innovation to home cooks everywhere, making the cooking experience smoother, cleaner, and more enjoyable.

As high school students passionate about technology and entrepreneurship, we're excited to solve real-world problems. Projecto Chef represents our commitment to innovation and our belief that great ideas can come from anywhere‚Äîeven from a messy kitchen!
